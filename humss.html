HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences)

The HUMSS strand is ideal for students interested in careers related to social sciences, communication, and public service.

HUMSS: The Power of Words, Ideas, and Society

Not all heroes wear lab coats or build machines—some change the world with words, ideas, and action. The HUMSS strand (Humanities and Social Sciences) is the path for those who seek to understand people, society, and the power of communication.

Why Choose HUMSS? The Heart of Society and Change

HUMSS students analyze human behavior, study cultures, and challenge the norms that shape our world. Whether it’s through law, media, education, or politics, HUMSS professionals drive change and progress.

The Benefits of Choosing HUMSS

  • Developing Strong Communication Skills: Express ideas with clarity and impact through writing, speeches, and debates.
  • Understanding Society and Culture: Gain insight into social structures, beliefs, and behaviors across the globe.
  • Endless Career Opportunities: HUMSS leads to careers in law, education, journalism, psychology, politics, and media.
  • Sharpening Critical Thinking and Problem-Solving: Analyze, interpret, and respond to societal issues effectively.
  • Influencing and Inspiring Change: Use your voice to advocate, inspire, and create meaningful impact.

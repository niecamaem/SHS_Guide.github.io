ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management)

The ABM strand is designed for students who want to pursue careers in business, entrepreneurship, and finance.

ABM: The Road to Business, Innovation, and Leadership

The world runs on ideas, strategy, and commerce—and the ABM strand (Accountancy, Business, and Management) is where future industry leaders, entrepreneurs, and financial experts begin their journey. If you have ever dreamed of starting your own business, managing a company, or mastering the art of finance, ABM is the perfect path for you.

Why Choose ABM? The Gateway to Success

Business is more than just money—it’s about innovation, leadership, and creating opportunities. The ABM strand develops skills in entrepreneurship, economics, marketing, and finance, preparing students for careers that drive the global economy.

The Benefits of Choosing ABM

  • Mastering Financial and Business Knowledge: ABM students dive deep into accounting, marketing, and business strategy.
  • Building Entrepreneurial Skills: Learn to develop, launch, and sustain a successful enterprise.
  • Developing Leadership and Management Abilities: Gain decision-making and problem-solving skills.
  • Diverse Career Opportunities: Careers in banking, corporate management, sales, marketing, finance, and more.
  • Learning Practical and Real-World Applications: Gain hands-on experience in business scenarios.
